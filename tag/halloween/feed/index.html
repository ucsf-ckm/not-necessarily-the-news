Monday, October 31, 12 – 1 pm
Parnassus Library / 5th Floor / Archives Reading Room

Join UCSF Archives and Special Collections on Halloween day to go behind the scenes to view select pieces from the historical collections in the UCSF Library 5th Floor Reading Room. You will see “medical oddities” of the past from surgical kits, bloodletting tools and more. The first 20 visitors will receive a goodie bag of treats!

Tweet your questions for our archives team from anywhere to @ucsf_archives and use #AskAnArchivist.

Archives Month (October 2016)

Join UCSF Archives & Special Collections in celebrating the value of historical records and the preservation of the past to the advancement of health worldwide. Keep tweeting us throughout the month @ucsf_archives with your comments and questions!
