Natural Standard, a popular database for natural and alternative medicine information, has merged with Natural Medicines Comprehensive Database to form a new database simply named Natural Medicines. As a former subscriber to Natural Standard, UCSF Library now has access to the newly enhanced database. Natural Medicines offers the best features and functionality from the two former products, as well as new clinical features that were not available before:

  • Natural ingredient monographs for over 90,000 commercial products
  • In-depth evidence tables and comprehensive review of evidence sourced from both databases and expert resources
  • Interaction, effectiveness, and adverse effects checkers
  • Patient handouts and consumer education monographs available in English, Spanish, and French
