The UCSF Library is happy to announce that access to PsychiatryOnline is available to our patrons. A powerful portal from American Psychiatric Publishing (APP), PsychiatryOnline notably features the electronic version of DSM-5, as well as an array of psychiatric references.

Search across a number of APP journals and textbooks, prepare for board certification and recertification with self-assessment tools, and easily take it on the go with their mobile friendly interface. Additionally, content is regularly updated: APA Practice Guidelines, Guideline Watches, and DSM codes are posted as soon as they are issued.
