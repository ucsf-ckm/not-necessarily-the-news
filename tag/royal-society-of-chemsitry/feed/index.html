UC authors can publish open access articles at no cost through 2016 in Royal Society of Chemistry (RSC) journals through RSC’s Gold for Gold program. This initiative offers the opportunity for UCSF researchers to publish their articles open access in RSC journals for free – both to authors and readers!

The UCSF Library has several RSC voucher codes and will assign them on a first-come, first-served basis. Voucher codes can be used to publish a communication, full paper, review, or technical note in an RSC journal under the publisher’s Gold OA option. Authors provide the code upon acceptance of their article in an RSC journal.

  • To request a voucher code, send an email to the Library, including the name of the author(s), journal title, and article title.
  • Once you have a voucher, submit the Gold for Gold Application Form.

This program will not be continued in 2017, so if you’re submitting to RSC, don’t miss this chance to go open access for free!
