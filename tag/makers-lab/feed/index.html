LOCATION: Parnassus Library Main Floor & 5th Floor

The Library invites you to an afternoon of openings to celebrate the upcoming Library exhibition, An Engine of Inquiry & Change: The UCSF Library and for a sneak peek of the soon-to-open Makers Lab.

Meet us on the main floor for knitting, coloring, 3d printing demos and more at the Makers Lab preview. Talk to a roaming Library expert to get your questions answered and learn more about how the Library serves students, faculty and staff. View silent films and artifacts from the Archives & Special Collections, then head to the 5th floor for a tour down memory lane with An Engine of Inquiry & Change: The UCSF Library, an exhibition chronicling the history of the UCSF Library and its users.

Light refreshments will be provided while supply lasts.
